Animals need help!

Why they need help?

  1. Animals too have a life. They too have feelings of hurt, pain and have every right to live.
  2. By helping them we gain their love and trust. They start living a fearless life because most of them feel that humans are gonna hurt them in some way or other.
  3. As animals, sometimes they are helpless as they cannot talk. We should understand their needs and help them accordingly.
  4. Just as we help humans, we should help animals too. They are not inferior.

Who are we?

  • We provide legal advice to citizens who have witnessed animal cruelty, our lawyers initiate and accompany criminal cases related to animal cruelty.
  • We save animals that have become victims of cruelty and war.
  • We provide food and medicine to shelters and volunteers who take care of homeless animals in the front-line areas of Ukraine.
